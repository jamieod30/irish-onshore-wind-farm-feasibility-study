# fid_7 Site Geometry Notes

## Overview

Parcel `fid_7` is the preferred developable land parcel identified during the GIS constraint screening stage of the project.

The parcel represents screened suitable land remaining after application of the active desk-study exclusion layers for buildings, roads, water features, ecological designations, and slope constraints.

## Geometry Characteristics

Visual review of the parcel indicates:

- one primary contiguous developable area
- irregular outer boundary
- broad central development zone
- constrained margins influenced by watercourses and local slope exclusions
- smaller disconnected suitable parcels nearby but outside the primary optimisation boundary

The parcel geometry is more suited to adaptive turbine placement than a rigid rectangular grid.

## Engineering Considerations

The parcel was screened using desk-study constraints only. Final development suitability would still require further review of:

- geotechnical conditions
- peat depth / bog stability
- drainage
- civil access design
- environmental assessment
- planning constraints
- construction logistics

## Project Role

Within this study, `fid_7` is treated as the primary parcel for constrained layout feasibility assessment and comparison against the original benchmark wind farm concept.
